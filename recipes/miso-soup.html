Miso Soup

Description

Miso Soup is a soup made primarily from dashi and miso paste, and may also contain ingredients such as tofu and wakame (seaweed)

Ingredients

  • Instant Dashi granules
  • Miso paste (white, red, or a mix)
  • Soft tofu, cut into 1/2in cubes
  • Scallion greens, thinly sliced
  • Garlic, Sliced (Optional)
  • Wakame (Optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat 2 cups of water in a small saucepan until simmering
  2. Add 1 tsp of instant dashi granules and mix until dissolved
  3. Add dried wakame and sliced garlic if using
  4. Add tofu and simmer until it is heated through, the garlic has mellowed out, and the wakame has reconstituted
  5. Turn off heat and mix miso into soup using a miso strainer
  6. To serve, ladle soup into bowls and top with sliced scallions
