Chicken and Sausage Gumbo

Description

Chicken and sausage gumbo is a classic cajun stew made with chicken, andoulle sausage, dark roux, and trinity. It is commonly served with rice and scallions.

Ingredients

  • Andoulle sausage
  • Bone in, skin on chicken thighs
  • Trinity + pope
  • Flour
  • Oil
  • Chicken stock
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in dutch oven until hot
  2. Add flour. Stir constantly over medium heat until a dark roux is formed. When finished, the roux should resemble a Hershey's Milk Chocolate bar in color.
  3. Add trinity to hot roux and stir. Cook until trinity has softened and sweat out most of it's moisture.
  4. Add garlic
  5. Deglaze with beer
  6. Add chicken stock, sausage, and chicken thighs
  7. Grind many grinds of pepper into the stew
  8. Cook until chicken thighs are done, about 2 hours. Remove chicken thighs.
  9. Cook for an additional time until burnt taste from the roux cooks out
  10. Shred chicken off of bone and add to stew
  11. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  12. Serve over white rice and top with scallions
