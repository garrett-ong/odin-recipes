Tamagoyaki

Description

Tamagoyaki is a rolled egg omelette flavored with soy sauce, sugar, and dashi. It is commonly served with rice. I also like to eat it with furikake or nori.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Instant dashi granules
  • Light soy sauce (Usukuchi Shoyu)
  • Sugar

Instructions

  1. Add light soy sauce, sugar, and instant dashi granules into a small bowl. Mix until dashi and sugar are dissolved.
  2. Add eggs to bowl with dashi mixture. Beat eggs until no streaks remain.
  3. Cook egg mixture in a pan over medium heat with oil. Roll into an omelette shape as you cook. Note: This is not easy and you will probably make scrambled eggs the first couple of times. It will still taste good.
  4. Serve with rice, furikake, and nori.
